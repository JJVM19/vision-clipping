Strategy call.

Frequently asked questions.

How is this different from just hiring an editor?
An editor gives you 3-5 posts a week. We can give you 100+ a day across every platform. One person can't do what a team of thousands does. And you're not managing anyone. We handle all of it.
What's the difference between an Internal Campaign and a Public Campaign?
Internal campaigns use a hand picked team of our top operators, posting to accounts you own and keep. Public campaigns open your brand to our wider editor pool filtered by the demographics you want, posting to their own accounts. Internal gives you ownership and control. Public gives you scale and reach across thousands of audiences you'd otherwise have to build from scratch.
Are the views real?
Every single view comes from a real person on a real platform. No bots, no fake traffic, no paid engagement. Our detection infrastructure catches botted views and non-organic traffic at the source, so everything on your dashboard is real engagement from real people.
How fast can you start?
Strategy locked within 3 days. We analyse your existing content and flag any additional assets that would sharpen the campaign. Editors matched by day 5, first posts live within 48 hours of that. Deposit tomorrow, your content is being distributed by the end of the week.
What if my content doesn't perform?
That's why we test at volume. We don't put one post up and pray. We test dozens of angles, hooks, and formats simultaneously across accounts. We find what works fast and kill what doesn't. If something's underperforming, we adjust the approach within the same week.
I already have a team doing this.
Good. How many posts are they putting out a month? If the answer is less than 30, you're leaving reach on the table. We also analyse what your team has already produced that worked, pull out what your audience actually responds to, and use that to optimise the rollout faster. Your team keeps their lane. We handle scale.
What do I actually need to provide?
Your existing content. That's it. YouTube videos, podcast recordings, webinar footage, whatever you already have. We handle the editing, the accounts, the posting, and the reporting. If you've got creative direction you want us to follow, send it over. Otherwise you don't do anything except check the numbers.
How does pricing work?
Three ways to work with us. Internal teams start at $5K/month plus a CPM budget (CPM is cost per thousand views, which funds the clippers producing your content). Public campaigns run at 30% of your total budget, so a $10K deposit means $7K to the campaign and $3K fee. Meta Ads amplification runs at 10% of ad spend. Once your organic campaigns have data, we re-engineer the best performing clips into highly targeted Meta Ads aimed at your ideal customer profile. Most clients start internal, prove what works, scale to public, then double down with Meta Ads. We walk you through what makes sense on the call.
Can I start with a smaller budget to test?
Yes. Most clients start with a 90-day pilot. Enough time and data to show real results. If it works, we scale. If it doesn't, you tested the channel at minimal risk. Most pilot clients end up scaling within the first 60 days.
Who's actually doing the work?
Not interns. Not AI. Emrah and Jaden direct every campaign personally, backed by a team trained over 3+ years on content creation and operations. Your content is handled by editors ranked on their performance in your specific niche. Underperformers get rotated out. Top performers get more volume. You get the best people on it at all times.
What happens when the campaign ends?
Everything we built is yours. The accounts, the followers, the content library, the data on what formats work for your brand. You can walk away with all of it. Or we keep the momentum going, scaling to public campaigns, layering on Meta Ads, or doubling down on your internal accounts. Either way, you own everything.
