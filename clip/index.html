The public pipeline

Five steps. That's the whole loop.

01

Join the community.

Hop into the Discord. New campaigns drop there, support lives there, announcements go out there.

02

Pick a campaign.

Read the info and rules, then look at active campaigns. Pick one that fits your editing style and the accounts you post from.

03

Download the footage.

Each campaign includes raw footage, b-roll, hooks, and any creative direction. Pull what you need and start cutting.

04

Clip and post.

Edit your clips and post them to the supported platforms listed on the campaign. Stay within the brand and content guidelines.

05

Submit and get paid.

Submit your post links through the dashboard. Views are tracked automatically. Payouts go through Whop.

Rules and brand safety

What's not okay.

Each campaign has specific brand and content guidelines, and you're expected to read them before clipping. The general rules below apply across every campaign on the platform.

No editing footage in a way that misrepresents the original content or the brand.

No view manipulation, bot traffic, or inauthentic engagement.

No content that's hateful, discriminatory, sexually explicit, or violates the platforms' terms.

Breaking these rules results in earnings withheld, account removed from the platform, and in serious cases a permanent ban from Vision Clipping campaigns. We monitor actively and move fast.

FAQ

Things people ask before applying.

Do I need a big following to clip with Vision Clipping?+

No. We pay for views generated by your clips, not for follower count. The algorithm rewards content quality more than profile size.

Can I clip from multiple accounts?+

Yes. Link every account you want to post from to your clipper profile, and views across all of them count toward your earnings. Just make sure each account meets the platform requirements of the campaign you're working on.

Can I work on multiple campaigns at once?+

Yes, on the public side. No cap on how many campaigns you can take. Internal clippers usually focus on one or two campaigns at a time because the base salary covers a defined output.

How fast do I get paid?+

Payouts depend on each campaign's Content Rewards system, but typically within 3 days of the cycle closing. Everything is processed through Whop.

Can I apply for the internal team after starting on public?+

Yes. A lot of internal clippers come from the public pipeline. If you're clipping consistently and your accounts are performing, that's exactly the track record we look for in applications.

What if my clips don't get views?+

You earn nothing on those clips, and that's the honest answer. Public clippers are paid purely on performance. The upside is unlimited but so is the downside if the work doesn't land. The fastest way to improve is to study the campaigns that pay, watch what's working on each platform, and iterate.

Do you work with clippers outside tier 1 countries (USA, UK, etc)?+

Yes. We work with clippers globally as long as we can legally send payment to your country.

Who owns the clips I make?+

The clips are made for the campaign and remain associated with the brand or creator who funded the campaign. You're paid for the views your posts generate, not for licensing or ownership of the clip itself.
