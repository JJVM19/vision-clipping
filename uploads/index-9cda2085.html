Frequently asked questions.

How does this compare to running paid ads?
Paid ads rent attention. Clipping builds a library that compounds. Most clients run both — we handle the organic leverage, your ads team handles bottom-funnel retargeting.
What happens if I don't have a lot of content?
Three hours of honest on-camera footage is enough for a first month. We'll run a recording-day playbook with you and walk away with 90–120 days of clippable material.
Are the views authentic?
100%. Every post is on-platform, on real accounts, surfaced by the algorithm to real humans. You can verify every number against platform-native analytics.
What results can I expect?
Clients on our core retainer average 8–30M views in month one, scaling to 30–120M by month three. Use the calculator above to project for your own inputs.
What platforms do you cover?
TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts and X by default. LinkedIn clips on request for B2B clients.
How does pricing work?
Monthly retainer plus an optional CPM pool. Retainer covers strategy, editing, publishing and reporting. The CPM pool is performance-based on top — pay for views delivered.

Personal Brand · Q2

Magnus Carlsen — chess content, engineered for gen-Z's feed.

Duration6 weeks
PlatformsTikTok · Reels · Shorts
Clips shipped184

Magnus came to us with a backlog of long-form interviews and streams — and an audience that skewed older than his category's growth edge. The brief was simple: put chess in front of a generation that doesn't open chess content.

Over six weeks, a matched squad of 14 editors cut 184 platform-native clips — pattern-interrupt hooks, reaction cuts, and visualizations of the board state tuned for vertical. We posted from three managed accounts with platform-specific captions.

The result: 67.6M views, 2.3M net-new followers and a top clip that did 12.4M on its own. The audience was 68% under-25 by the end of the campaign.
